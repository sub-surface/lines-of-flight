# Lines of Flight

> *"A line of flight is not made of two points. It passes between points, it comes up through the middle."*

A minimal, meditative game about a dot that stays and a line that leaves.

Built with vanilla JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas. No dependencies, no build step.

## The Seed

A black dot sits in the middle of a white screen. That is the whole world, at first.

You move your mouse and a trail follows — not a cursor, but a **living line** that you have drawn into being. The trail is permanent. It has weight. It sags, it swings, it remembers.

There are two beings here:
* **The Dot (Stillpoint)** — rooted, sedentary, the condition for departure.
* **The Line (you, becoming)** — the trail your hand draws. Always in passage.

The game is the long conversation between staying and leaving.

## Philosophy

Inspired by *Thomas Was Alone*'s geometric characters with interior lives, but grounded in the philosophy of Gilles Deleuze and Félix Guattari:
* **Line of flight** — escape and becoming.
* **Smooth vs. striated space** — fields of forces vs. rigid boundaries.
* **Plateaus** — intensities without climax.
* **Assemblages** — connection as the only real mechanic.

## Features & Implementation

* **Fluid Vector Field Dynamics:** Curl-noise base coupled with dynamic attractors, repellers, vortices, and shivers.
* **Diegetic Typography:** Stillpoint's voice precipitates as physical ink glyphs that hold their form, dissolve under wind, and flow away to merge with the rest of the ink.
* **High-Performance Batched Canvas Rendering:** Collapses thousands of individual particle draw calls into quantized alpha/width Path2D batches.
* **Zero Dependencies:** Single-page design, easily deployable as a static asset.
